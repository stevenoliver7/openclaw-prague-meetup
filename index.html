🤔 What is OpenClaw?

Short version: Your personal AI assistant that runs on YOUR machine.

It's an open-source framework connecting AI models to your tools — GitHub, Gmail, calendar, browser, files. You customize it. You control it.

What makes it special:

• Skills system — Add capabilities via markdown files
• Multi-model — Claude, GPT, Gemini, or local
• Privacy-first — Your data stays local
• Actually useful — Not a demo, a daily tool

📋 The Agenda

5 parts, ~3 hours, zero fluff:

1. Free Talk & Introductions

Open floor — meet the people around you.

2. Structured Discussion Round

Guided questions: What's your setup? Your skills? Which models do you run? Miro board for collective discussion.

3. Energy Round

Shooting ideas, sharing setups, producing energy together. Open mic style.

4. 2 × Keynotes (~30 min each)

Inspiring talks from people who built something genuinely useful or cool.

📣 Submissions open! DM us to present.

5. Hands-On Building

Bring your laptop. Everyone leaves having built or improved something.

📣 Want to Present?

2 Keynote Slots Open

~30 min each. Show us something you built that genuinely saves time, improves a business, or is just really cool.

Looking for: Real projects, honest stories, practical takeaways. Not sales pitches.

❓ FAQ (The Honest Version)

"I don't know OpenClaw. Is this for me?"

Yes. Part 5 is literally "let's get you set up." Bring a laptop, leave with it running.

"I'm not technical enough."

If you can follow a README, you're technical enough. Half the value is meeting people.

"Is there food?"

We're in a pub. Food/drinks available for purchase. Czech beer ~$2-3.

"What language?"

Main talks in English. Smaller conversations in whatever works.

"Is it worth my time?"

If you want to meet people who actually BUILD things — yes. If you want passive lectures — probably not.
