## Luma

### Event Name
OpenClaw Prague Meetup #1 — The AI Meetup That Doesn't Suck 🐱

### Date & Time
Thursday, March 26, 2026 · 17:30 – 00:00 CET

### Location
Prague, Czech Republic (Exact venue TBD — will be emailed to RSVPs)

### Description

**The first OpenClaw community meetup in Europe. Open-source AI tinkerers sharing what they've built — real code, no sales pitches, good beer.**

We're building on open-source AI agents. Come tinker, show us what you broke this week, and meet the other people in Prague who stay up until 3am because "wait, what if I just tried this..."

### The Agenda

- **17:30 – Open Talk:** Grab a beer, find a seat, and let's get introduced.
- **18:00 – Lightning Demos:** Two short shows of what people have built (and maybe broke). Real code, no sales pitches.
- **19:00 – Late – Chat & Hacker Hours:** Build things yourself, debug with the group, explore ideas together.

### Who's This For?

Developers, data scientists, ML engineers, and AI tinkerers who actually build things. If you've shipped a bot, fine-tuned a model, or broken something interesting — you'll fit right in.

### Speakers Wanted

We're looking for 2 speakers for ~20-minute talks. Show us something you built that's cool and maybe even useful. We want weird, real stories and in-depth tech deep dives. DM us on Telegram to submit.

### Your Host

**Daniel Vecera** — Senior Data Scientist, decade of experience from energy trading to ML. Currently shipping Transformer-based recommender systems at TV Nova. Previously co-founded RichView AS (AI-generated industry reports in the pre-ChatGPT days). Started this meetup to find the other people in Prague who get obsessively excited about this stuff.

### Registration

⚠️ Limited to 50 tinkerers. To keep the signal-to-noise ratio high, please include two short sentences about who you are and what you're tinkering with.
