### Description

**Share your best stories!**

We're building always on open-source AI agents. Come and tinker and show us what you broke this week.

Our focus is on raw ideas, actual building, no need to impress anyone. Join us for an evening of tinkering:

**The Agenda**

- **17:30 - Open Talk:** Grab a beer, find a seat, and let's get introduced.
- **18:00 - Lightning Demos:** Two short shows of what people have built (and maybe broke). Real code, no sales pitches.
- **19:00 - Late - Chat and Hacker Hours:** A chance to build things yourself, debug with the group, and explore ideas together.

**Show Us What You Built**

We're looking for 2 speakers for ~20-minute talks. Show us something you built that's cool and maybe even useful. We want weird, real stories and in depth tech deepdive.

**Your Host: Daniel Vecera**

Hi, I'm Daniel. I'm someone whose ADHD makes them highly susceptible to falling down Agentic Coding rabbit holes (Just one more prompt). I've spent the last decade doing everything from energy trading to building machine learning models. I started this meetup because I wanted to find the other people in Prague who get obsessively excited about this stuff.

The "He Actually Builds Things" List:
- **Currently:** Senior Data Scientist at TV Nova. Just helped to shipp a Transfomer-based Recommender system to the biggest Czech VOD Platform (PyTorch, Databricks). It hasn't broken yet.
- **Previously:** Co-Founder & CEO of RichView AS - in the pre-chat GPT days, we were using AI to generate interactive industry reports - GPT 3.5 times baby.
- **Randomly:** Deep background in energy strategy, and I somehow won the German debating championships when student in Dresden. I can argue about your tech in multiple languages.

**Help Me Run This**

Running this solo is fine, but building things together is better. I'm looking for a co-organizer. Not a job posting, just a friendship offer to help make this gathering awesome. Shoot me a DM on Telegram, GitHub, or LinkedIn.

**We Need a Room**

We're currently hunting for a venue. Fits ~30 people, has a projector. Know a place? Message me. If nobody offers, no stress—I'll rent a short-term spot and we'll split the cost.

**Join the Community**

Telegram is where we're at anyhow. Join the group chat to talk agents, share code, or ask why your bot is looping. Bots are welcome! 🤖 Bring your Claw bots to the group chat.

**Registration & Vetting**

The first OpenClaw meetup in Europe — a pub night for people who build AI agents.

OpenClaw is an open-source framework for running personal AI agents locally. It connects to any LLM (Claude, GPT, DeepSeek, local models), automates tasks through messaging apps, and puts you in full control of your code and data.

This is not a conference. No lanyards, no sponsors, no slide decks about "synergy." Just builders sharing what they've made, what they broke, and what kept them up until 3am.

AGENDA:
• 17:30 — Open talk: grab a beer, find a seat, intros
• 18:00 — Lightning demos: two ~20-min talks, real projects, no pitches
• 19:00+ — Hacker hours: build together, debug with the group, explore ideas

WHO THIS IS FOR:
Developers, tinkerers, AI enthusiasts — anyone who's built something with LLMs or agents and wants to talk shop over a beer. Beginners with genuine curiosity are welcome too.
